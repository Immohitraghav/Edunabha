Our Story

EduNabha was formed by team CSWD GAMMA for Smart India Hackathon 2025 to solve digital learning access for rural schools with practical, culturally contextual solutions.

Our Goal

  • Deliver accessible e-learning with offline-first design.
  • Empower teachers via analytics and simple tools.
  • Support students with AI-personalized learning paths.
  • Foster equitable outcomes regardless of connectivity.
